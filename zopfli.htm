About UGUI: Zopfli

UGUI: Zopfli is a GUI for the command line application Zopfli. Zopfli performs brute force attempts to losslessly compress PNGs. The more iterations it performs the more likely it is to reduce it's size. This is by far the slowest, but also the most effective method of PNG compression.
